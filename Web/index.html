Help

Camera stream

Pressing the "Start stream" button will start the stream from the camera. Likewise, "Stop stream" will stop the stream from the camera.

Keyboard control

The default method for platform control is via keyboard. You can navigate by holding 'w', 's', 'a' or 'd' to move forward, backward, turn left or turn right. When you release these keys the platform will stop moving.

Myo control

You can also control the platform via Myo armband. You need to have "Myo Connect" program installed on your computer, and the Myo Bluetooth dongle inserted. When you sync the armband, the input toggle will be enabled and you can switch to Myo control.
The platform will move forward, backward, turn left or turn right when you make hand poses 'fingers-spread', 'fist', 'wave-in' and 'wave-out'. The platform will stop when you stop making hand poses.

Additional commands

Expanding the right sidebar will enable acces to additional commands.
You can switch the "Lights" toggle to turn on the four LEDs on the platform.
By switching the "Distance" toggle you will be shown the last distance measured by the platform's ultrasonic sensor. The sensor will make measurements of up to 1 meter and the distance will update every 500ms.
You can also toggle the lights by pressing the 'f' key and show measured distance by pressing the 't' key. Pressing the spacebar will stop the platform in both keyboard and Myo control mode. Pressing 'j' and 'k' will expand or collapse left and right sidebars.
